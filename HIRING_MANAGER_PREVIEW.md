## ğŸ¨ VISUAL DESIGN LANGUAGE

### **Color Psychology:**
- **Blue (#0066CC)**: Trust, technology, professionalism
- **Green (#00CC66)**: Innovation, growth, success
- **Orange (#FF6B35)**: Energy, creativity, action
- **Dark Gray (#2C3E50)**: Stability, expertise

### **Typography Hierarchy:**
- **H1**: Bold, 48px - Page titles
- **H2**: Semi-bold, 32px - Section headers
- **H3**: Medium, 24px - Subsection headers
- **Body**: Regular, 16px - Content text
- **Small**: Regular, 14px - Metadata, captions

### **Spacing & Layout:**
- **Generous whitespace** for breathing room
- **Consistent margins** (16px, 32px, 48px)
- **Card-based design** with subtle shadows
- **Rounded corners** (8px border-radius)
