## ME102B - Mechatronics: Robotic Fish

### Project Overview

This project was completed as part of ME102B (Mechatronics) coursework at UC Berkeley. Designed a unique tail mechanism for a robotic fish, earning second place at the design showcase.

### Key Achievements

- Designed and implemented a unique tail mechanism for robotic fish locomotion
- Earned second place at the design showcase
- Integrated mechatronic systems including actuators, sensors, and control systems
